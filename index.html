Who We Are

We are a group of Security & Design driven individuals passionate about installing CCTV Cameras & we are committed to make your Facility well Secured.

Our group was formed in September 2019, when our Team Leader Mr. John Carlo D. Soliven decided to put up his business in Cauayan City, in our early stage as a group we already installed 14 different projects in 12 different locations, all projects having the same goal. "To be clear, secured & to be safe." With our group expertise and the high quality brand cameras we have, we can establish a well secured facility, house, condo, open or closed areas.

Define

We define problems as we go on our site surveys, we see to it that all concerned areas and location will be focused by our security cameras. Searching for the electrical wires to be well in placed, main lines, sub lines and pipe required wires.

Design

After we finish our site survey, we plan our design for your security cameras. Brainstorming to make the best plan and place each camera in it's required location and as we continue the design, we will prepare all the required materials to make the project successful. Upon creating the plan & design we hope to meet the clients expectation.

Install

When the planning & design is complete, our group together with our installers will start to install the CCTV cameras. Camera location and Cameras main focus will be our first target, the wires will be placed accordingly, the main lines will have it's way to the recorder and the counter measures for heavy environment weather will commence as we test the cameras. Hopefully, we will finish the project with the exact given time.

Commit

After all these things are done, our group will stay committed to the project. It's what we call "After Sales Service", we value this kind of service to maintain a good relation the client, upon your call we will give you an on call support & on site support. Just schedule the date of your availability and our group will fix everything for your camera to work again. We promise these things as our group is looking for a good feedback of our clients and as well as their recommendation.

Simple Design

The design of the camera is very unique and simple, with it's latest technology you can beat the night vision of common cameras as the brands we use holds the newest technology that features clear & colored vision in the night! playbacks will be easily identified using these kind of cameras.

Strategy

Together with the team, our strategy is to finish the project with the given period of time. We cater time management & we always stick to the plan to successfully install all the cameras before the deadline. We use standard hardwares to protect our cameras & wires to prevent rustiness. Our main line is well created and our cameras/recorder is well installed and well configured.

UI Design

The User interface is very simple and user friendly, we configure the recorders and cameras in the most friendly state. 20-30 days of recording depending on the Hard disk, Slides for proper monitoring, high definition cameras and viewing, our device is very easy to use to provide comfortment towards the client.

Mobile Development

User friendly, easy to use, easy to handle, and easy to manage. Take a screenshot, mobile recording or two way audio are the main features of our cameras.
